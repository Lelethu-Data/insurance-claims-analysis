# Insurance Claims Risk & Fraud Analysis

### Project Overview
Analyzed 6 months of insurance claims data to identify cost drivers, regional risk patterns, and fraud exposure. Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to surface insights and recommend executive actions for proactive cost control.

### Tools & Skills
Power BI, Excel, Data Analysis, Data Visualization, Business Intelligence, Risk Analysis, Fraud Detection, Data Storytelling, SQL

### Key Findings

| **Metric** | **Value** | **Insight** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Highest Risk Region** | Gauteng: R 2,340,380 | 30%+ of total claim cost |
| **Primary Cost Driver** | Accident claims: R 3,701,370 | 48.49% of all claims |
| **Fraud Exposure** | R 1,400,000 / 40 cases | KZN = 25.68% of fraud cases, R 491,270 loss |
| **Monthly Volatility** | Apr R 1.30M → May R 1.04M → Jun R 1.57M | 50.9% spike May–June |

### Executive Summary
Gauteng carries the highest regional cost at R 2,340,380. Total claims are driven primarily by Accident claims, which account for 48.49% of all claims at R 3,701,370, and Fraud at 18.86% with R 1,400,000 across 40 cases. KwaZulu-Natal presents the greatest fraud risk, responsible for 25.68% of all fraud cases and R 491,270 in losses. Claim amounts showed a sharp drop from R 1,300,000 in April to R 1,040,000 in May, followed by a steep rise to R 1,570,000 in June. The root cause is high Accident frequency in Gauteng combined with concentrated fraud in KwaZulu-Natal. To address this, the business should strengthen Accident claim audits in Gauteng and launch a dedicated fraud unit for KwaZulu-Natal, while investigating the April–June volatility. Management should set an acceptable monthly claim ceiling, which I will monitor through dashboard KPI alerts that trigger when exceeded.

### Recommendations Delivered
1. **Targeted Audits**: Deploy enhanced Accident claim audits for Gauteng to reduce frequency-driven costs
2. **Fraud Unit**: Establish dedicated fraud investigation team for KwaZulu-Natal  
3. **Anomaly Investigation**: Review May–June volatility for operational or fraud drivers
4. **Executive Dashboard**: Implement KPI alerts for monthly claim totals vs. risk ceiling
